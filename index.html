What are apples?

Apples are the most common and grown fruits in the world, they are round shaped fruits that grows on apple trees, and they are usually red, green and yellow. Apples have a crispy texture and has a sweet and tangy taste and can be used for juices, pies, and sauces.

Apple Nutrients
