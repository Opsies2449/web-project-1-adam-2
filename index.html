What are apples?

Apples are the most common and grown fruits in the world, they are round shaped fruits that grows on apple trees, and they are usually
red, green and yellow. Apples have a crispy texture and has a sweet and tangy taste and can be used for juices, pies, and sauces.

Health benefits of apples

Eating apples have lots of healthy benefits like;

  1. Better digestion and gut health because of fibres that eats bacteria.
  2. Helping with blood sugar because of the fibres that helps with sugar absorption.
  3. Supporting heart health because of pectin that lowers cholesterol.
  4. Antioxidants like quercetin to prevent inflammation.
