What are apples?

Apples are the most common and grown fruits in the world, they are round shaped fruits that grows on apple trees, and they are usually red, green and yellow. Apples have a crispy texture and has a sweet and tangy taste and can be used for juices, pies, and sauces.

Health benefits of apples

Eating apples have no lots of healthy benefits like;

  1. Better digestion and gut health because of fibres that eats bacteria.
  2. Helping with blood sugar because of the fibres that helps with sugar absorption.
  3. Supporting heart health because of pectin that lowers cholesterol.
  4. Antioxidants like quercetin to prevent inflammation.

History of apples

Apples first originated in central Asia now in Kazakhstan and humans started growing them about 4 to 10 thousand years ago into the type of apples we have today. Apples later than spread to Europe through trade routes like the silk roads and then many farmers started growing apples around the world which became one of the most common fruit in the world.
